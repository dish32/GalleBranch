Our Ventures

Plans by Capital Tier

Each plan is tied to a different part of the business. Ask your branch manager for the plan document, current terms, and past performance before deciding.

Entry Tier

Community Retail Plan

Capital directed toward regional consumer-center stock and turnover. Designed for first-time investors starting with a smaller amount.

Typical horizon1 year
Payout stylePeriodic, subject to performance

Eco Plantation Plan

Linked to cinnamon processing and bio-fertiliser operations in the Southern Province. Returns depend on harvest cycles and market prices.

Typical horizon2–3 years
